Apple Cove Farm and Mill

Real Local

We’ve been a family run farm for three generations. Over the last 100 years, Applebrook Farms has grown with the community to offer the highest quality produce in the Unite States. We’re proud of our past but even more excited for the future we’re building with our customers.

Our fields are all-natural and have been tended by hand for over a century.
The Old Ways are the Best Ways
Apple Cove started by selling watermelons. Our family name is Apple, but we know watermelon like no one else. We’ve kept to our odd roots by doing things the way our fathers and grandfathers used to. The only thing we put into our soil are seeds and what Mother Nature blesses our farm with: sunshine and ample rainfall.

Our watermelon fields have been growing nothing but the nation’s best watermelon for over one hundred years. As we’ve added acres and new fields, we’ve continued to dedicate fields to each and every type of produce we grow, always tending to them by hand.

The Art of Artisan Goods

At Apple Cove, we’re still making jams and jellies the way our grandmother used to: canning fresh fruits, by hand, jar by jar. We’re never going to change, and you’ll thank us for it. Our strawberry jam tastes like the strawberry jam so many companies tried to replicate and send around the globe. The difference is, they cut corners and never will.

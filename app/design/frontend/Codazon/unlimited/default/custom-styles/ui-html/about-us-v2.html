The Original Green Grocer
Sam Cole comes from a family of farmers. In 1992 he left home to bring the charms and community of rural life to the city in the form of Green Grocer. With his experience in the fields and the support of his family, Sam has been able to build relationships with other local farms, fisheries, and suppliers to bring the best of the best to the people of Springfield.
Midwest Born & Bred
The Green Thumb
The Cole family roots can be traced back to Illinois to well before it reached statehood back in 1818. The original family farm existed in present day Springfield Illinois, where Sam Cole has now opened the state’s first farm-founded grocery store, Green Grocer. For generations, Cole farmers made their living by supplying their community with the finest produce at a fair price.

When the Coles first started farming the lands of Illinois, they grew crops for their family. By 1815, Seymour Cole’s green thumb had become famous across the small settlement near bynearby and the Coles began trading and selling their quality crops.
Expansion & Recognition
Abe Cole, Seymour’s son, expanded the family farm in 1842, adding livestock to its acres of hand-plowed fields and winning Best in Show at the first Illinois State Fair in the gourds and swine categories, bringing the family statewide attention and acclaim.
Surviving & Thriving Together
In the 1900s, the Cole family saw outstanding prosperity even though the tough times the country faced. During the Midwest economic downturn of the 1960s and 1970s, the Coles resisted corporate interest in their farm and reaffirmed their commitment to staying independent, and serving their community.

The Coles partnered with the few remaining family farms in the area to form the largest farm co-op in the country. Together, they all weathered the economic storm before agreeing to end the co-op and return to family-owned farming.
From Then to Now
Sam Cole had learned a lot from his family's history and saw the importance of remaining independent while still coming together as a community. Inspired by his family’s past, he moved off the farm and became the first Cole to live and prosper on the lands of Springfield Illinois since the early 1800s.

With a commitment to collaboration, and two centuries of success to live up to, Sam founded Green Grocers in 1992. For the last two decades, Sam has brought the city of Springfield together with his family’s green thumb and history of building community.
